Our Story

Founded with a vision to accelerate India's technological advancement, Curiosity Arc Foundation emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: that research and innovation should directly benefit society.

Today, we stand as a bridge between academic excellence and real-world impact, funding and executing research that addresses India's unique challenges while contributing to global sustainability goals.

Our Vision

To be India's leading catalyst for technology research that drives sustainable development and societal impact.

  • Bridge research and implementation
  • Create lasting social impact
